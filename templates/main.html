What is it?

This is a remix of my own twitter timeline. I generally keep my twitter account locked. So, its like a weird window into what I'm saying, but not exactly what I'm saying. You can visit it (me?) here: @n_remixed.

How To Use It

You don't. It just tweets randomly a few times a day. Sometimes there is a photo culled from my flickr archives. Sometimes there is not. It operates off a markov based library, so its trying to sound like me. Which is sorta weird. I've tried to get it to use a large timeline sample, so its not just trying to remix the latest 200. In a way this is a remix of a lot of the processes I used to make the tumblr freud bot as well.

Why Make This?

I had an assignment called remix. I figured the best thing to remix was myself. I like the idea that I could just be a bot. So I made myself into a bot. But there's also some weird questions about identity in there. Like, all my tweets, every last one, for 10 years...is 16mb. That's pretty small, considering I poured a lot of feelings / brains, etc into the random twitter void. It makes me think about how small our Big Data files really are.
